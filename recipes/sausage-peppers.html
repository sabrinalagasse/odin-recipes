Sausage Stuffed Peppers

Description

Sausage stuffed peppers are the perfect blend of spicy, creamy, and indugent. Perfect as a game day appetizer or for dinner with a side salad!

Ingredients

  • Jalapenos
  • Cream cheese
  • Jimmy Dean pork sausage
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Wash and dry peppers, cut in half and remove the seeds. Place peppers on baking sheet.
  4. Cook pork sausage in a large pan until browned.
  5. While sausage is still hot, mix in the cream cheese and shredded cheese until fully combined.
  6. Spoon in sausage mixture into each pepper half.
  7. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes or until filling is browned and bubbling,
  8. Let cool for 5 minutes and enjoy!
