Zuppa Toscana Soup

Description

This recipe is a copy cat for the beloved Olive Garden special. Best served with a side of crusty bread!

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock
  • Hot italian pork sausage
  • Potatoes --Yukon gold or whatever your perference
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • Heavy cream/half and half/milk
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Kale
  • Bacon bits (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and dry produce. Dice onion and mince garlic. Quarter potatoes. Remove kale from stems and tear/chop into bite sized pieces.
  2. In a large pot, cook sausage until browned. Remove from pot, drain fat and set aside.
  3. Add onion to pot and cook on medium week until semi-translucent. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add stock to the pot and bring to a boil. Once boiling, add potatoes and bring back to a low boil until potatoes are tender.
  5. Add cooked sausage, kale, and bacon bits if using to the pot. Lower to a simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Turn off heat and ladle in bowls. Top off with cream or milk to your liking. Enjoy!
